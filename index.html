Welcome

CrossFit Midnight Life located in Albury NSW, focus on Consistency, Community and Passion. New WOD’s (Workout Of the Day) are posted every day on our app. The WOD is always constantly varied, with functional movements that prepare you for life, performed at an intensity that is suitable for your level.

Facilities

CrossFit Midnight Life are not your stock standard commercial gym. Here at our gym in Albury, you won’t find treadmills, cable machines or mirrors. We focus on functional movements; drawing from the foundations of Olympic weightlifting techniques, gymnastics, body weight exercises and cardiovascular exercises like running, rowing and skipping. We use barbells, bumper plates, kettle bells, medicine balls, dead balls, climbing ropes, pullup bars, gymnastics rings and plyo boxes just to name a few.
